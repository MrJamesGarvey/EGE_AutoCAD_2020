Orthographic Projection Introduction

 





What is an Orthographic Projection?

 

An Orthographic projection is a 2-D representation of a 3-D object. The 2-D drawing represents different sides of an object.

 

 

The six principal views

 

The 6 principal views are created by looking at the object (straight on) in the directions indicated.
