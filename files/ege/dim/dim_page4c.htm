Feature Dimensions

 





Different types of features require unique methods of dimensioning.

 

  1. If space is at a minimum, the size and location of a radii may be given by using a false center and jogged dimensions.

 

 





  1. Solid parts that have rounded ends are dimensioned by giving their overall dimensions.

         

             - If the ends are partially rounded, the radii are also given.

         

         

     - For fully rounded ends, the radii are indicated but the value is not given.

 

 

  1. Slots are dimensioned by giving their overall dimensions. Or, by giving the overall width and the distance between centers. The radii are indicated but the value is not given.
