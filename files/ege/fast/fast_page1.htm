Fasteners

 





Fasteners include:

 

  • bolts and nuts (threaded)
  • set screws (threaded)
  • washers
  • keys
  • pins

 

Fasteners are not a permanent means of assembly such as welding or adhesives.

 

 

Fasteners and threaded features must be specified on your engineering drawing.

 

  • Threaded features: Threads are specified on a detailed drawing in a thread note.
  • General Fasteners: Purchasing information of a general fastener is given on a standard parts sheet to allow the fastener to be ordered correctly.
