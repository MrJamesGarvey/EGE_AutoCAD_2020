Metric Threads

 





Metric Thread Tables

 

Standard screw thread tables (ASME B1.13M-2001) are available in order to look up the:

 

  • Major diameter
  • Pitch
  • Minor diameter or Tap drill size.

 

Thread tables are located in the Appendix of the Engineering Graphics Essentials book.
