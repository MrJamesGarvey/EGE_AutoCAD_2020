When developing an idea or a design concept, the assembly comes first. However, once the design is complete and you are ready to draw the final working drawing package using a computer drawing software, the assembly drawing is usually completed last. This is simply out of convenience. The assembly drawing is simply constructed by assembling the already drawn parts.
