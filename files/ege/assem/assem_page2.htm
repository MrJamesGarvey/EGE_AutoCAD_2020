Components of an Assembly Drawing





Assembly drawing views

 

Do assembly drawings need a FRONT, TOP and RIGHT SIDE view? Sometimes. We need as many views as it takes to identify and locate each part. It may only take one view.

 

Part identification

 

A part is located and identified by using a circle or balloon containing a find number and a leader line that points to the corresponding part.

 

Parts list / bill of material

 

The parts list is an itemized list of the parts that make up the assembled machine. A parts list may contain, but is not limited to what is shown in the figure.
